MOC mocp Keyboard Shortcuts

The most important Beginner-Key for MOC is ? or h to show the Shortcut-Help-Screen.


MOC = Music on Console.

A Music Player for the Linux Commandline that runs with JACK - Yeah!


To start MOC just hack this into your console: mocp
